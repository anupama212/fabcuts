About The App

Discover the best salon near you, join the queue via Fab Cuts, The Salon App.
Whether you're at school, busy at work or lounging at home, join the queue, sit back and watch your position in the queue so you can leave just at the right moment to slide straight onto the chair with Fabcuts.
Fabcuts offers a platform for customers to discover barbershops in their area, the shop's location, opening time and customer reviews. Barbershops can also add additional information and photos to their salon, including link of their social media pages. Customers are able to view the number of customers in barber’s shop on the app and barbers can send notifications to the customers in their queue updating them on the estimated time.
Each salon show which barbers are online and allows customers to choose which Barber they would like to get cut. The barber’s profile shows their reviews and rating, photo gallery and current queue length. After their hair cut, customers can rate the hair cut and the Salon. In this way, barbershops and their barbers can ensure that they remain busy throughout the day, as potential customers sitting at home can see that there is a short queue or that the queue is moving quickly. Further, barbershops and barbers can increase their customer base by receiving excellent customer reviews.
Having new technology may seem a bit unnecessary for a salon but we’re here to tell you that’s not the case. Making the customer happy can mean a lot more than just the cuting. By making the service as streamlined and as pleasant as possible, customers are encouraged to come back for their next cut.
By enabling customers to join their favorite barbervqueue at the office or at home, the customer waste less time waiting in the salon. Customers are much more likely to join the queue on the app.
Further, the Fabcuts app ensure delivering the best possible service in the most effective way.
